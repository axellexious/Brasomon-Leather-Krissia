About

Brasomon is a handcraft leather brand from Philippines. It started in 2015 and became well known throughout the province for more than 4 years, and has branches in every town.

Quality

Brasomon selects and combine good and fine materials so the handcrafts can be aesthetic and elegant like how the customer wanted it to be.

Service

Brasomon is dedicated to outstanding service that adds a elegant fashion to the person who use it.

Design

The Brasomon design is inspire by Flower of Heidelberg that will inspire all Filipinos to become fashionable using the brasomon leather.
